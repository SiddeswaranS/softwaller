Each of these industries has workflow demands — yarn lot tracking, gold-rate-linked billing, BOM-driven work orders, job-card-based foundry production — that boxed software either ignores or charges crippling customisation fees to bolt on.

Industries We Serve in Coimbatore

Coimbatore is not one market — it is at least five overlapping clusters, each with its own grammar. We have built software for every one of them.

Textile (Spinning, Weaving, Processing, Garment)

Spindle-level production capture, yarn lot and shade lot traceability, count-wise stock valuation, dyeing batch records with shade-card matching, fabric inspection (4-point system), and Tirupur-style job-work in/out flows for fabric, stitching, embroidery, washing and finishing. Material balance per challan, rejection capture, rate cards by process, and consolidated job-work GST invoices.

Jewellery (Wholesale & Retail)

Live gold and silver rate from your bullion API or rate-board entry, design-wise wastage and making charges, BIS hallmark and HUID logging, RFID tag-level stock for daily counter audit, scheme management (gold deposit schemes), GST tax invoice with HSN 7113, and old-gold purchase against new sale.

Pump & Motor Manufacturing

Multi-level BOM with sub-assemblies, work orders by route card, ancillary supplier portal for tier-1 and tier-2 vendors, GRN with quality inspection, serial-number traceability for warranty claims, and dispatch-wise dealer dashboards. Built for OEMs and ancillaries supplying KSB, Texmo, ELGI, Suguna and similar buyers.

Foundry & Engineering

Heat-number traceability from melting to dispatch, sand-mix and chemistry records, machine-wise OEE, job-card-based production for machine shops, batch quality with rejection root-cause, and sub-contractor job-work for grinding, plating and heat-treatment with GST e-invoicing.

Wet Grinder, Motor & Appliance Units

Peelamedu and Sundarapuram cluster units making wet grinders, mixers, pumps and appliances need finished-goods serial tracking, dealer-wise dispatch, AMC and warranty registration over WhatsApp Business, and field-service apps for installation and complaint resolution.

Agro & Auto Ancillary

Pollachi and Mettupalayam supply chains for agro equipment, sprayers and auto components run on tight margins. We build vendor portals, kanban supply schedules, e-invoice integration with OEM portals, and quality records aligned to IATF 16949 expectations.

Can your jewellery billing software pull live gold and silver rates?
Yes. Our jewellery billing module fetches gold, silver and platinum rates from your preferred bullion API or your manual rate-board entry, applies wastage and making charges per design, supports BIS hallmark and HUID capture, prints GST tax invoices, and tracks RFID stock at item level for daily counter audit.
