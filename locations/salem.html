Frequently Asked Questions — Salem

Answers to what Salem business owners ask us before signing up.

Where is your Salem office?
Our registered office is at 3-15/8 Singandi Lakshmana, Mudhali Street, Tharamangalam, Salem, Tamil Nadu 636502. We meet clients across Salem city, Suramangalam, Hasthampatti, Five Roads, Yercaud Main Road and the Tharamangalam industrial belt.
Do you visit our Salem factory for requirements gathering?
Yes. For Salem, Mettur, Attur, Tiruchengode and Namakkal clients we visit your factory or office in person within 30 to 60 minutes for the first requirement walk-through, no charge. Subsequent on-site visits during development and go-live are also included.
Can you support Tamil-language users?
Yes. Our Salem team speaks Tamil and English. We can train factory supervisors, store keepers and billing operators entirely in Tamil, and the software UI can show Tamil labels for key fields where needed.
How is custom software different from off-the-shelf accounting software for sago or steel units?
Off-the-shelf accounting software handles books and GST but does not understand sago grade-wise yields, steel coil-wise weighbridge entries, mango lot grading or transport trip-sheets. Custom software from Softwaller models your actual factory floor, integrates weighbridges and barcode scanners, and still posts clean entries to your accounting software.
What is the typical timeline for a Salem SME project?
A typical Salem SME project goes live in 6 to 12 weeks: 1 to 2 weeks for on-site discovery, 4 to 8 weeks for development and customization, 1 week for data migration and Tamil-English user training at your factory, and 1 week of hand-holding go-live support.
