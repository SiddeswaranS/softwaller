Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions buyers ask before choosing this software.

How is search faster than just using a shared Google Drive or OneDrive?
We index document content using OCR, so searches find text inside scanned PDFs, photos, and old DOC files. Metadata fields (project, client, type, date) and saved searches cut document retrieval time from minutes to seconds.
Does it support version control and audit trail?
Yes. Every edit creates a new version with the editor's name, timestamp, and change summary. Older versions are restorable. Full audit logs cover who viewed, downloaded, edited, or shared each file.
Can we set permissions by folder, document, or department?
Yes. Role-based access works at folder, sub-folder, document, and even page level for PDFs. Departments only see what they need; sensitive documents (HR, legal, finance) stay restricted.
Is it compliant with DPDP Act and ISO 27001 norms?
Yes. We support encryption at rest and in transit, India data residency, consent capture for personal data, retention policies, audit logs, and the access controls required by DPDP Act 2023 and ISO 27001.
