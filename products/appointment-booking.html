Seamless Scheduling for Every Business

12 powerful features to fill your calendar and delight your customers.

  1. 01

    Online Booking

    Let customers book 24/7 from your website or app. No phone calls needed — bookings come in while you sleep.

  2. 02

    Calendar Sync

    Sync with Google Calendar, Outlook, and Apple Calendar. Avoid double-bookings with real-time availability.

  3. 03

    SMS / Email Reminders

    Automated reminders to reduce no-shows by up to 80%. Customizable timing and message templates.

  4. 04

    Payments

    Collect booking fees or full payment at the time of booking. Integrated with popular payment gateways.

  5. 05

    No-Show Tracking

    Track no-shows and manage cancellation policies. Identify repeat offenders and protect your revenue.

  6. 06

    Multi-Location

    Manage bookings across multiple branches or locations. Centralized view with location-specific availability.

  7. 07

    Service Catalog

    List services with duration, pricing, and availability. Customers pick exactly what they need before booking.

  8. 08

    Staff Scheduling

    Assign appointments to staff based on skills and availability. Balance workloads automatically.

  9. 09

    Recurring Bookings

    Set up weekly, monthly, or custom recurring appointments. Perfect for regular clients and ongoing services.

  10. 10

    Waitlist Management

    Auto-notify customers when slots become available. Fill cancellations instantly without manual follow-up.

  11. 11

    Custom Forms

    Collect intake information before the appointment. Custom fields, dropdowns, and file uploads for any workflow.

  12. 12

    Booking Analytics

    Bookings per day, revenue by service, peak hours analysis. Data-driven insights to optimize your schedule.
