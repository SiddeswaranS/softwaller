Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions buyers ask before choosing this software.

How does online booking reduce no-shows?
Automated SMS and WhatsApp reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before the appointment cut no-shows by 30 to 50 percent in our customer base. Customers can also reschedule from the reminder link without calling.
Can it sync with Google Calendar and Outlook?
Yes. Two-way sync with Google Calendar, Outlook, and Apple Calendar means staff see all appointments in one place and external blocks (lunch, holidays) reflect as unavailable in booking.
Does it handle multi-staff and multi-location businesses?
Yes. Each staff member has their own schedule, services, and availability. Customers pick a location first, then a service, then a staff member, all in one booking flow.
Can customers pay during booking?
Yes. Razorpay, Cashfree, PhonePe, and UPI payment links are integrated. You can set full payment, partial advance, or pay-at-venue per service type.
