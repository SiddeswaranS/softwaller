Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions buyers ask before choosing this software.

Is CostWise free to use?
Yes. The core CostWise app is free on Android and iOS for personal expense tracking, budgets, and reports. Premium features for advanced analytics and multi-account sync are available as in-app upgrades.
Does CostWise sync across multiple devices?
Yes. Premium users get encrypted cloud sync across phones, tablets, and a future web dashboard. Free users keep all data securely on the device.
How is my financial data protected?
All data is encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit with TLS 1.3. We never sell, share, or analyze user data for ads. You can delete your account and all data at any time from in-app settings.
Can I export my expense data?
Yes. Export to CSV, Excel, or PDF anytime. Premium users also get filtered exports by category, account, date range, and tag, plus auto-monthly email reports.
