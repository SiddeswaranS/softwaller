Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions buyers ask before choosing this software.

Is your billing software GST and e-invoicing compliant?
Yes. Our billing software generates GST-compliant tax invoices, IRN through registered GSPs, e-way bills, and returns data ready for GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B. It supports B2B, B2C, exports, SEZ, and reverse-charge transactions.
Do you support multi-state and multi-GSTIN businesses?
Yes. Each branch can have its own GSTIN with separate invoice series, place-of-supply rules, and consolidated reporting at the head-office level. Inter-branch transfers and ITC reconciliation are automated.
How does it integrate with Tally or our existing accounting?
We provide two-way sync with Tally Prime via XML or ODBC, and direct integration with Zoho Books, QuickBooks, and SAP. You can keep your accounting tool while using our billing for GST and operational entries.
What happens if e-invoice IRN generation fails?
The system retries automatically, queues failed invoices, and alerts your accounts team. We handle every common IRP error code, from duplicate IRN to invalid HSN, with one-click fixes and re-submission.
