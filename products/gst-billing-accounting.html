Complete Financial Management

12 features to handle every aspect of billing, accounting, and GST compliance.

  1. 01

    GST Invoicing

    100% GST-compliant invoices with HSN/SAC codes. Generate professional invoices in seconds.

  2. 02

    E-Invoice

    Generate IRN via NIC portal with QR code on invoice. Fully automated e-invoicing compliance.

  3. 03

    E-Way Bill

    Auto e-way bill generation for goods movement. Create, update, and cancel e-way bills from within the system.

  4. 04

    Bank Reconciliation

    Match bank statements with ledger entries automatically. Identify discrepancies in minutes, not hours.

  5. 05

    GSTR Export

    Export GSTR-1, GSTR-2A, and GSTR-3B returns in JSON format. File returns directly from the system.

  6. 06

    Accounts Receivable

    Customer aging reports, automated payment reminders, and receipt tracking. Collect faster, every time.

  7. 07

    Accounts Payable

    Vendor bill management, payment scheduling, and TDS deduction. Never miss a payment deadline.

  8. 08

    Ledger Management

    Create and manage unlimited ledger accounts. Organized chart of accounts with group hierarchy.

  9. 09

    Journal Entries

    Record adjustments, provisions, and year-end entries with full narration and document attachments.

  10. 10

    Multi-Currency

    Handle transactions in multiple currencies with automatic exchange rate updates and gain/loss calculation.

  11. 11

    Cash Flow Reports

    Daily, weekly, and monthly cash flow statements. Know exactly where your money is going.

  12. 12

    TDS Compliance

    Auto TDS calculation, Form 26Q generation, and certificate issuance. Complete TDS lifecycle management.
