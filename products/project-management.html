Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions buyers ask before choosing this software.

Does it support Gantt charts, Kanban, and time tracking?
Yes. Each project supports Gantt, Kanban, list, and calendar views with drag-and-drop. Time tracking runs per task with manual entry or live timers, and converts to client invoices automatically.
How does it handle client billing for project work?
Time entries roll up to billable hours, fixed-fee milestones, and retainers. Invoices generate automatically with GST, TDS, and currency conversion. Clients pay via integrated Razorpay and Stripe links.
Can clients see project progress in a portal?
Yes. A white-labeled client portal shows milestones, completed tasks, blocked items, screenshots, and invoices. Clients comment on tasks and approve deliverables without needing to log into your main system.
Does it integrate with Slack, Teams, and Google Workspace?
Yes. Notifications, daily standup posts, and task creation work from Slack and Microsoft Teams. Google Drive and OneDrive attach files directly. Calendar invites flow to Google and Outlook.
