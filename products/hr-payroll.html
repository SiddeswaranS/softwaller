Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions buyers ask before choosing this software.

Does your payroll software handle PF, ESI, PT, TDS, and Form 16?
Yes. Statutory components are computed automatically every payroll cycle: PF (12 percent up to wage ceiling), ESI (0.75 percent employee + 3.25 percent employer), state-wise PT, TDS as per slabs, and year-end Form 16 generation.
How does attendance integrate with payroll?
Attendance flows from biometric devices, mobile GPS check-in, RFID, or face recognition into a single dashboard. Leave, overtime, late-coming, and shift premiums automatically reflect in salary calculations.
Is your software DPDP Act compliant for employee data?
Yes. We follow DPDP Act 2023 norms: explicit consent capture, role-based access, audit logs, encryption at rest, India data residency, and a defined data retention and deletion policy.
Can employees see payslips and apply for leave on mobile?
Yes. The Employee Self-Service (ESS) app on Android and iOS gives access to payslips, Form 16, leave application, attendance correction, IT declaration, and reimbursement claims.
