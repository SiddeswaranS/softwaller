Complete Stock Control

12 features to eliminate stockouts, reduce waste, and optimize your supply chain.

  1. 01

    Multi-Warehouse

    Manage stock across unlimited warehouses with real-time visibility into every location's inventory.

  2. 02

    Barcode / QR Scanning

    Scan items for receiving, picking, and dispatch. Eliminate manual errors and speed up operations.

  3. 03

    Auto Reorder

    Automatic purchase order generation when stock hits reorder level. Never run out of critical items.

  4. 04

    Batch Tracking

    Track items by batch number with full traceability from supplier to customer for recalls and compliance.

  5. 05

    Supplier Reports

    Supplier performance, lead time, and quality reports. Make informed decisions about your supply partners.

  6. 06

    Expiry Management

    FEFO-based picking, expiry alerts, and auto-holds. Minimize waste and ensure product freshness.

  7. 07

    Stock Transfer

    Inter-warehouse transfer with transit tracking. Move stock between locations with full audit trail.

  8. 08

    Stock Valuation

    FIFO, LIFO, and weighted average valuation methods. Accurate stock value for financial reporting.

  9. 09

    Cycle Count

    Scheduled cycle counts without shutting down operations. Maintain accuracy without full physical audits.

  10. 10

    Serial Number Tracking

    Track individual items by serial number. Know the exact history and location of every unit.

  11. 11

    Low Stock Alerts

    Real-time notifications when stock falls below threshold. Stay proactive, not reactive.

  12. 12

    Returns Processing

    Manage customer and supplier returns with reason tracking. Full visibility into return trends and causes.
