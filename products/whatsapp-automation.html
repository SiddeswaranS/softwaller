Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions buyers ask before choosing this software.

Do I need WhatsApp Business API or is the regular Business app enough?
The free WhatsApp Business app suits businesses with under 100 messages per day from one phone. Above that, or if you need broadcasts, chatbots, multi-agent inbox, or CRM/ERP integration, you need WhatsApp Business API through a Meta Business Solution Provider.
How much does WhatsApp Business API cost in India?
Meta charges per conversation: marketing conversations are around Rs 0.78 each, utility around Rs 0.115, authentication around Rs 0.115, and service is free for the first 1,000 per month. BSP markup adds Rs 0.20 to 0.50 per conversation typically.
Can your bot handle Tamil, Hindi, and other Indian languages?
Yes. Our chatbots support Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Marathi, Bengali, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, and English with automatic language detection from the customer's first message.
Will my number get banned for sending broadcasts?
No, if you use the official API and follow Meta's rules: pre-approved templates, opted-in recipients only, low spam-report rate, and respecting the 24-hour service window. We help configure all of this correctly.
