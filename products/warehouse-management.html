Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions buyers ask before choosing this software.

What is the difference between WMS and inventory management?
Inventory management tracks how much stock you have. WMS tracks where exactly each item is (bin, rack, level), how it moves (putaway, picking, packing, dispatch), and who handled it. WMS is essential once you cross 5,000 SKUs or run multi-bin warehouses.
Does WMS support wave picking and zone picking?
Yes. We support batch picking, wave picking, zone picking, and cluster picking. The system picks the most efficient strategy based on order profile, picker availability, and warehouse layout.
Can WMS integrate with our ERP and shipping partners?
Yes. WMS syncs orders, stock, and dispatch data with ERPs (SAP, Tally, Oracle, Zoho) and connects to shipping APIs (Delhivery, Bluedart, Shiprocket, Shadowfax) for label generation and tracking.
Does it work with handheld scanners and tablets?
Yes. Operators use rugged Android devices or tablets for receiving, putaway, picking, and packing. Voice-directed picking and pick-by-light hardware are supported for high-throughput warehouses.
