Streamline every inch of your warehouse — from receiving to shipping. Real-time inventory, barcode scanning, and optimized workflows to maximize throughput and minimize errors.

Complete Warehouse Control

12 powerful features to run your warehouse with precision and speed.

  1. 01

    Pick / Pack / Ship

    Optimized workflows with pick lists and packing slips. Reduce errors and speed up order fulfillment from shelf to truck.

  2. 02

    Bin Locations

    Organize your warehouse into zones, aisles, racks, and bins. Every item has a home for instant retrieval.

  3. 03

    Barcode Scanning

    Scan items at every step for accuracy and speed. Eliminate manual data entry and reduce picking errors to near zero.

  4. 04

    3PL Integration

    Integrate with third-party logistics providers seamlessly. Sync orders, inventory, and shipping across partners.

  5. 05

    Returns Processing

    Manage inbound returns with inspection and restocking workflows. Track return reasons and process refunds efficiently.

  6. 06

    Put-Away Rules

    Auto-suggest bin locations based on product type and velocity. Intelligent slotting to minimize travel time.

  7. 07

    Wave Planning

    Group orders into waves for efficient batch picking. Optimize pick paths and reduce warehouse travel time.

  8. 08

    Inventory Accuracy

    Cycle counts, adjustments, and variance reports. Maintain 99%+ inventory accuracy without full shutdowns.

  9. 09

    Receiving & QC

    Goods receipt with quality inspection checkpoints. Verify quantities, check conditions, and flag discrepancies instantly.

  10. 10

    Cross-Docking

    Direct transfer from receiving to shipping dock. Skip storage for fast-moving items and reduce handling costs.

  11. 11

    Labor Management

    Track worker productivity, pick rates, and efficiency. Identify top performers and optimize workforce allocation.

  12. 12

    Dashboard & Reports

    Real-time warehouse KPIs, throughput, and utilization metrics. Make data-driven decisions with actionable insights.
