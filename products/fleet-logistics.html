Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions buyers ask before choosing this software.

What hardware do I need for fleet tracking?
AIS-140 certified GPS devices for commercial vehicles in India, plus optional fuel sensors, temperature sensors, and dashcams. We work with hardware partners or you can use existing devices that support standard protocols.
Can it detect fuel theft and unauthorized stops?
Yes. Capacitive fuel sensors detect sudden drops outside fuel-station geofences. Engine-on without movement, route deviation, and after-hours use trigger immediate WhatsApp and SMS alerts to fleet managers.
Does it integrate with FASTag and toll data?
Yes. We pull FASTag transactions per vehicle, reconcile with route plans, flag mismatched tolls, and feed cost data into trip P&L for accurate per-trip profitability.
Can drivers use a mobile app for trip and POD management?
Yes. Drivers receive trip assignments, capture POD with photo and e-signature, log expenses, raise issues, and complete vehicle inspection checklists from a simple Android app that works offline.
