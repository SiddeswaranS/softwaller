Complete Field Force Management

12 powerful modules to track, manage, and optimize your field sales and service operations.

  1. 01

    GPS Visit Tracking

    Real-time location tracking with geo-fenced check-in and check-out to verify field visits automatically.

  2. 02

    Mobile Order Booking

    Place orders on-the-go with a full product catalog, customer-specific pricing, and instant order confirmation.

  3. 03

    Expense Claims

    Submit expenses with photo receipts and auto-approval workflows to streamline reimbursement processing.

  4. 04

    Daily Reports

    End-of-day reports with visit summary, orders placed, collections made, and key highlights for managers.

  5. 05

    Offline Mode

    Full functionality without internet connectivity, with automatic data sync when connection is restored.

    Route Planning

    Optimized daily routes to minimize travel time and fuel costs with intelligent sequencing algorithms.

  7. 07

    Beat Planning

    Assign territories and routes to field reps with weekly/monthly schedules and coverage tracking.

  8. 08

    Collection Tracking

    Record cash and cheque collections in real-time with instant reports for accounts and management.

  9. 09

    Customer Visit History

    View past visits, orders, payments, and feedback at each customer stop for informed conversations.

  10. 10

    Leave & Attendance

    Mark attendance via GPS location, apply for leave from the app, and track team availability in real-time.

  11. 11

    Photo & Note Capture

    Capture site photos, customer feedback, and detailed visit notes with timestamps and location tags.

  12. 12

    Manager Dashboard

    Real-time view of all field reps, their locations, visit status, and performance metrics on a single screen.
