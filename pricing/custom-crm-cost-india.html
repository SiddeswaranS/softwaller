Custom CRM Development Cost in India — Complete Pricing Guide (2026)

A direct, no-fluff answer: a custom CRM in India costs between ₹1,50,000 and ₹25,00,000 as a one-time build in 2026. A small 10–20 user sales team starts at ₹1.5–3 lakh, a typical 30–100 user SME CRM lands at ₹3–8 lakh, and large enterprise rollouts with multi-team workflows, AI scoring and complex integrations run ₹8–25 lakh. Compared with SaaS CRMs that charge per user every month forever, custom CRM is a one-time spend plus 18–22% AMC. Most Indian sales teams crossing 25 users hit the SaaS-vs-custom break-even between month 12 and month 30, after which custom is dramatically cheaper. This guide breaks down every line item, a 3-year TCO comparison against Zoho CRM, Salesforce Sales Cloud, HubSpot Sales Hub and Freshsales using their official 2026 list pricing, the cost drivers that move the quote up or down, the hidden SaaS fees most buyers miss, and a free RFP template you can send to any vendor.

5. What Drives Cost Up vs What Drives Cost Down

If your budget is fixed, here is the playbook for making it stretch — and the traps that quietly inflate quotes by 40% or more.

Drives Cost Up

  • Scope creep mid-build — adding modules after sign-off triggers rework cascades.
  • Bespoke UI everywhere — custom-designed every screen vs reusing a design system.
  • Unstable integrations — connecting to legacy systems with no APIs (screen-scraping, file dumps).
  • Real-time sync requirements — sub-second propagation to mobile, ERP and BI simultaneously.
  • Heavy compliance needs — DPDP, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, RBI guidelines.
  • White-label multi-tenancy — running your CRM as a product for resellers.
  • 24×7 SLA — pager rotation, multi-region failover, hot-standby DB.

Drives Cost Down

  • Tight scope, fixed feature list — written PRD beats verbal wishlist.
  • Reusing design system — adopt vendor's existing UI library; skip pixel-level redesign.
  • Phased rollout — Phase 1 core CRM, Phase 2 marketing, Phase 3 BI.
  • Web-first, mobile later — defer the mobile app until web is validated.
  • Standard integrations only — pick vendors with documented REST APIs.
  • Fewer roles & permissions — 3 roles is much cheaper than 12.
  • Cloud-managed infra — Azure/AWS managed services beat self-managed VMs.

6. Hidden Costs in SaaS CRMs to Watch

Sticker prices on SaaS CRM pricing pages rarely reflect the real bill. Here are six recurring traps Indian buyers report after 6–12 months on Zoho, Salesforce, HubSpot or Freshsales.

Hidden CostWhat It Looks Like
1. Per-user fees creepYou start with 20 users at ₹1,500/month. Two years later you have 70 users and the bill is ₹12.6 lakh/yr — every new hire adds to the subscription.
2. Advanced features paywalledWorkflow automation, custom roles, advanced reports, AI scoring — all gated behind Enterprise or Ultimate tiers that cost 2–4× the entry plan.
3. Integration markups"Premium connector" fees, marketplace add-ons, third-party plugins (e.g., document-gen, e-sign) billed separately at ₹500–3,000/user/month each.
4. Customization premiumNeed a 7th custom field, a non-standard pipeline stage or a unique report? You either upgrade the plan or hire a partner at ₹1,500–3,000/hour.
5. Onboarding & training"Implementation packages" charged at ₹50,000–₹3,00,000 separate from the subscription — and renewed yearly as Customer Success retainers.
6. Data export / leave feesAPI rate limits, export caps, attachment-download fees and migration-out-charges that punish you for switching vendors after years of use.

Custom CRM eliminates all six. You pay once for the build, the source code is yours, every field/report/role is open to change, and there is no "leave fee" because you already own the data and the database.

Frequently Asked Questions

The seven questions Indian buyers ask us most before signing a custom CRM contract.

How much does custom CRM cost in India for a 30-user team?
For a 30-user sales team, expect ₹3,00,000 to ₹6,00,000 as a one-time build, depending on modules, integrations and whether a native mobile app is included. Compared with Zoho CRM Enterprise at ₹2,400/user/month (₹8.64 lakh/yr for 30 seats), custom CRM pays back inside the first year.
When does custom CRM beat Zoho or Salesforce on cost?
For teams of 25 or more users, custom usually wins on 3-year TCO. Break-even versus Zoho Enterprise is around 12–18 months; versus Salesforce Sales Cloud Enterprise, break-even is typically 4–8 months because Salesforce Enterprise lists at US$165/user/month. The smaller your team and the more standard your sales process, the more SaaS makes sense — the larger and more specialised your team, the more custom wins.
Can I migrate data from Zoho or HubSpot to a custom CRM?
Yes. Standard CSV exports from Zoho CRM, HubSpot, Salesforce, Freshsales and Pipedrive are imported with a documented field-mapping spreadsheet. We dedupe, validate, do a UAT cycle, then a final cutover. Attachments and call recordings are migrated via API or bulk download depending on the source CRM.
Is mobile app included in custom CRM cost?
In our SME and Enterprise tiers, a native Android + iOS field-sales app is part of the standard scope. For the Basic tier, the mobile app is an optional add-on starting at ₹75,000 — though the web app is fully responsive and works on tablets and phones in every tier.
What's the typical AMC for custom CRM?
Annual Maintenance Contract is 18–22% of the one-time build cost. AMC covers bug fixes, server monitoring, security patches, OS / framework updates, scheduled backups, restore drills and a defined SLA for Sev-1/Sev-2 issues. Net-new feature development is billed separately at agreed rates.
Do I own the source code?
Yes. With Softwaller, you own 100% of the source code, database schema, infra config and design assets after final payment. Code is delivered on your Git server (GitHub, GitLab, Azure DevOps, Bitbucket) and there is no licensing dependency on us. You can self-host on your AWS, Azure or on-premise environment with zero permission needed.
How long does custom CRM development take?
Basic CRM goes live in 4–6 weeks. SME CRM with full sales + marketing modules and a mobile app takes 8–12 weeks. Enterprise CRM with multi-team workflows, AI scoring and complex integrations takes 12–20 weeks. We deliver in 2-week sprints with demos every Friday so you see progress continuously.
