Every module ships pre-wired for GST, e-invoicing, and audit trails. Pick what you need on day one and add the rest in phase 2.

  1. 01

    BOM & Engineering

    Multi-level BOM explosion, alternate items, ECN/ECO version control, drawings attached at item or operation level, phantom assemblies, and where-used reports for engineering changes.

  11. 11

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from manufacturing buyers evaluating custom ERP.

How is custom ERP cheaper than SAP B1 for our 80-user plant?
For an 80-user plant, branded ERP licence + implementation usually lands between ₹40L and ₹90L over three years once you add user fees, AMC, hosting, customisation, and consultant rates. A right-sized custom ERP for the same scope typically costs ₹20L to ₹40L all-in. The savings come from one-time build cost, no per-user licence, no consultant lock-in, and modules scoped only for what you actually use on the shop floor. From year 4 onwards you pay AMC only.
Can the ERP collect data from CNC/PLC machines on the shop floor?
Yes. We integrate with PLCs and CNC controllers over OPC-UA, Modbus TCP, or MQTT to capture cycle counts, downtime, and reject signals. For shops without machine-level integration, we deploy ruggedised tablets or barcode scanners at each workstation so operators can log start, stop, idle, and reject reasons in Tamil or Hindi. Both methods feed the same OEE and production tracking dashboards.
Does it support IATF 16949 / ISO 9001 / GMP audit requirements?
Yes. The quality module includes incoming, in-process, and final inspection checklists, SPC charts, NCR/CAPA workflows, calibration records, and document control with version history. Audit trails capture who changed what and when. For pharma we add GMP electronic batch records, deviation logs, and 21 CFR Part 11-style e-signatures. For auto components we add PPAP, control plans, FMEA, and supplier scorecards aligned to IATF 16949.
How does subcontracting / job-work flow work?
The ERP creates a job-work challan against an open work order, debits stock to the subcontractor's account, tracks ageing per the GST 1-year/3-year rules, generates the e-way bill, and posts back receipts when the processed material returns. Process loss, free-issue components, and subcon costing are all reconciled against the parent BOM. This eliminates the manual Excel reconciliation most plants do today and keeps your ITC clean.
Can we have Tamil and Hindi UI for shop-floor operators?
Yes. We deliver shop-floor screens — work order list, job card, quality entry, downtime reasons, scrap codes — fully translated to Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, or Marathi. Each user picks their preferred language at login. Office and management screens stay in English so reports and audits remain consistent.
How long does data migration from existing system take?
Master data migration — items, BOM, vendors, customers, opening stock — typically takes 3 to 6 weeks depending on data quality. We provide cleanup templates, run dry imports against your UAT instance, and reconcile balances with finance before go-live. Transactional history is usually kept in the legacy system for read-only reference rather than imported in full, which keeps the new system lean.
Do you provide on-site support post go-live?
Yes. Every manufacturing implementation includes 6 months of on-site or near-site support after go-live, with an engineer present at your plant during the first 4 weeks. After that we move to a standard AMC with defined SLA tiers, monthly health reviews, and quarterly enhancement sprints.
